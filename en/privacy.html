Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

1. Introduction

KineticMCP ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your data when you use our software and website.

2. Data Collection & Self-Hosting

KineticMCP is a self-hosted software. This means:

  • We do not have access to the data you process through your KineticMCP instance.
  • Your Salesforce credentials, data, and API keys remain on your infrastructure.
  • We do not collect telemetry or usage data from your self-hosted instances unless explicitly opted-in (currently no such feature exists).

3. Website Data

When you visit kineticmcp.github.io, we may use standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand visitor traffic. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

4. Artificial Intelligence

KineticMCP facilitates connection between your data and AI providers (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI). We are not an AI provider. Your data is sent directly from your infrastructure to the AI provider you configure. You are responsible for complying with their data policies.

5. Contact

For any privacy concerns, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
