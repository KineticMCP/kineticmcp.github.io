Sales Ops: Pipeline Hygiene

Problem: Reps forget to update Close Dates or Next Steps. Reports are always wrong.

Action: Agent scans open opportunities every Friday. Identifies stale dates. Reads recent emails/calls to infer status. Updates the record and flags "At Risk" deals.

Result: 100% accurate pipeline visibility, zero manual data entry.

RevOps: Cross-Stack Sync

Problem: Changing a pricing tier in Stripe requires manual updates in Salesforce Products.

Action: Agent monitors Stripe webhook. When a product changes, it upserts the Pricebook Entry in Salesforce and notifies the Sales team channel.

Result: Perfect sync without brittle Zapier scripts.
